Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

Rabbit Hole Publisher is a personal content management tool used to publish video content to TikTok. This application is operated by an individual for their own use and does not collect, store, or process data from any third parties.

2. Data Collection

This application does not collect personal data from any users, as it is not a public-facing service. The only data accessed is the account holder's own TikTok account information, which is required to authenticate and publish content via TikTok's API.

3. TikTok Account Data

When connecting to TikTok, the application accesses the following through TikTok's official API with the account holder's explicit consent:

— Basic profile information for authentication purposes
— Video upload and publishing capabilities
— Account analytics for content performance tracking

This data is only used to facilitate content publishing and is not shared with any third parties.

4. Data Storage

Any data processed by this application is stored securely on private infrastructure controlled by the account holder. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with TikTok's API. TikTok's own privacy policy governs how TikTok handles data on their platform. No other third-party services receive user data from this application.

6. Data Retention

Authentication tokens and content metadata are retained only for as long as necessary to operate the publishing service. The account holder may revoke access at any time through TikTok's account settings.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated at any time without prior notice.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the application owner via the associated TikTok account.
